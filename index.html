The flowering of the guayacanes is a phenomenon that occurs once a year and for a period of time of a few days.Once a year Ecuador lives a unique natural spectacle with the flowering of guayacanes, dry forest trees located in few areas of the country that are dressed in intense gold, perfuming the environment and embellishing the landscape and is planned for this weekend

when?

The flowering of these trees occurs in January after the first winter rains of the year, which also announce the beginning of planting. The growth of the guayacanes is possible between 200 and 1,000 meters above sea level and for a tree to reach 15 meters in height it is necessary to wait up to 200 years.
